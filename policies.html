Appointment Guidelines

Please ensure all eye makeup and oils are removed prior to your appointment. Clean lashes allow the adhesive to bond perfectly for longer-lasting results.
Lash appointments take time! Wear something cozy so you can fully relax and even take a "lash nap" while I work my magic.
Caffeine can cause your eyelids to flutter, making application difficult. Please avoid these drinks at least 4 hours before your session.
Please arrive with your hair tied back in a ponytail and contact lenses removed to avoid irritation and ensure safety during the service.
Every eye shape is unique! Bringing reference photos helps me understand your desired style so we can customize the perfect set for you.
